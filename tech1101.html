VIRAT KOHLI

About his life:

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is a renowned Indian cricketer. He exhibited exceptional talent from an early age and quickly rose through the ranks of Indian cricket. Kohli made his international debut in August 2008 against Sri Lanka and has been a pivotal figure in the Indian cricket team ever since.

Kohli's meteoric rise can be attributed to his unwavering dedication, passion, and tireless work ethic. He is recognized for his aggressive style of play and remarkable consistency across all formats of the game. His adeptness in batting and excellent fielding skills have earned him numerous accolades, including the prestigious Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Cricketer of the Year.
